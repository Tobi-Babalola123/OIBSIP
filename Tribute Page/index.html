A Loving Tribute to Pastor Sunday Babalola

Honoring the life of a devoted servant of God

About Pastor Sunday Babalola

Pastor Sunday Babalola, born in Oyo State, Nigeria, was a devoted Christian and pastor who dedicated his life to spreading the gospel and serving his community. He was a loving father who instilled strong faith in his children, ensuring they grew closer to God.

He was a man of great discipline, never late to church, and he always led by example. He lived by the motto: "Don't follow multitude."

Key Achievements

  • Devoted pastor who served God faithfully.
  • Successful career as a battery charger, owning five cars and building a house.
  • Community secretary for the Community Development Association (CDA), contributing to societal improvement.
"Don't follow multitude."

- Pastor Sunday Babalola

His Legacy

Pastor Sunday Babalola left an indelible mark on everyone who knew him. He was a beacon of light in his community, loved both at home and abroad. His integrity and dedication to both his faith and profession continue to inspire those who were fortunate to cross his path.

Though he passed away in 2015 at the age of 56, his presence is felt every day, offering peace and comfort. His legacy lives on through his family and the lives he touched.
